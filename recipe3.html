Ingredients

1 Servings
  • 2 cups Rice
  • 1 cup Urad Dal
  • 1 tsp Fenugreek Seeds
  • 0.5 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 tbsp Oil

Steps

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for at least 4 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked ingredients separately and then mix them together with salt and water to form a batter.
  3. Let the batter ferment overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  4. Heat a tawa or non-stick pan, pour a ladle of batter, and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosai.
  5. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden brown. Flip and cook the other side if desired.
  6. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
