Poori Masal

★★★★☆ Based on 357 ratings
78.3k Save Share In app
Venkatesh Bhatt Renowned South Indian Chef www.kitchenstories.com
Difficulty Easy ✌️
Preparation 10 min
Cooking 10 min
Resting 0 min

Ingredients

1 Servings
  • 4 Idlis
  • 2 tbsp Podi (Spiced Powder)
  • 2 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1 tsp Urad Dal
  • 1 pinch Asafoetida
  • 1 sprig Curry Leaves

Steps

  1. Cut the idlis into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat sesame oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, urad dal, asafoetida, and curry leaves.
  3. Once the mustard seeds splutter, add the idli pieces and toss well.
  4. Sprinkle the podi over the idlis and mix thoroughly until the idli pieces are well-coated.
  5. Serve hot with chutney or sambar.
